When I have free time, I like to:

Read

Reading is my favourite past time. I enjoy romance, fantasy, supernatural, adventure or sci-fi fiction stories.

Most of the time I read fanfiction about my favourite books or movies on Fanfiction.net. Other times I like to read blogs or interesting articles online.

Sometimes I write my own stories for fun.

Draw

A quick drawing I did a while ago for fun while listening to relaxdaily Youtube channel. I find it easier to be inspired when listening to instrumental music.

I've been drawing ever since I was a young girl. This is a talent that I hope will complement my future career in Web Design.

Vist the Beach

When the weather is nice one can often find me (provided that there is an acceptable number of people, I just hate it when the beach is crowded) with my mp3 player, humming to myself, while feeling the soft sand swallow my sun-kissed feet as I stroll along the shoreline of Milnerton Beach.
